Venue Information

South Shore Cultural Center
7059 S South Shore Dr
Chicago, IL 60649

Getting There

From the conference hotel:

The hotel is a short walk from a METRA station. South Shore Cultural Center is 5 stops south with the station adjacent to the SSCC. The METRA runs every 26 minutes.

The hotel will also have shuttles but they'll be shared with other guests.

Public Transportation:

Use the City of Chicago's planner and specify South Shore Cultural Center as the destination.

Parking:

South Shore Cultural Center has paid lot for parking should you decide to drive.
